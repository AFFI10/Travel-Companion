Qutub Minar

Roundtrip from Rs79

Description:Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Delhi, India, is a towering symbol of Indo-Islamic architecture. Standing at 73 meters, it is the world's tallest brick minaret, built in the early 13th century by Qutb-ud-din Aibak. The intricate carvings and calligraphy on its sandstone surface showcase the rich heritage of the time..

Lotus Temple

One-way from Rs39

Description: Lotus Temple, another architectural marvel in Delhi, is a Bahá'í House of Worship known for its distinctive lotus flower shape. Completed in 1986, this modern and awe-inspiring structure welcomes people of all faiths to pray and meditate in a serene environment. The Lotus Temple has become an iconic symbol of unity and religious diversity..
